## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Per-section background gradients

Each section SHALL have a unique background gradient using the existing color palette.

#### Scenario: Hero background
- **WHEN** the page loads
- **THEN** the hero section SHALL display a radial gradient with neon-violet at 20% left

#### Scenario: Services background
- **WHEN** the page loads
- **THEN** the services section SHALL display a subtle linear gradient with celestial-light

#### Scenario: Cases background
- **WHEN** the page loads
- **THEN** the cases section SHALL display a radial gradient with twilight colors

#### Scenario: CTA background
- **WHEN** the page loads
- **THEN** the CTA section SHALL display both neon-violet and whisper-blue gradients

#### Scenario: Testimonials background
- **WHEN** the page loads
- **THEN** the testimonials section SHALL display a distinct neutral gradient
